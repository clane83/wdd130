Our Adventures

Big Bend

Looking for a fun river run with friends? Big Bend is our Class III and fun for the whole family. Work as a team to get through the many rapids with 2 White Water secions. All skill levels are welcome.

Ranchero

Want a more leisure rafting trip? Ranchero is our Class II and fun for the whole family. Work as a team to get through the many rapids. All skill levels are welcome.

Cowboy

Looking for more difficult rapids? Cowboy is our Class IV, we recommend only skills rafters. Work as a team to get through the many rapids with many White Water secions. Not recommends for beginngers.
